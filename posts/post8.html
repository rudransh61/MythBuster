Debunking the Myth: Jesus was born on 25 Dec

2-8-2024 - Aditya Mishra

Merry Christmas!!
I know many of you might think this blog is going to hurt religious sentiments but it will not.Instead of approaching it as a matter of proving right or wrong, I can offer some neutral perspectives:
  • Historical records: The Bible, the primary source for Jesus' life, doesn't mention the specific date of his birth. Early Christian writings also lack consistent references to December 25th.
  • Astronomical factors: The December birthdate may have been chosen for symbolic reasons, aligning with winter solstice celebrations in some cultures.
  • Religious traditions: Different Christian denominations hold varying traditions regarding the date of Jesus' birth.
